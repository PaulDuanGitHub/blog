Blog 1: Proper noun

Feeling Of Reading

When I knew we were going to read a novel, actucally I was worried if I had the same ability as native students to read a book in English. After I read a few chapters, I fond it was not so hard for me to finish teacher’s reading task. I even could feel a little proud of myself.

Proper Noun

I was not surprise that this book had some proper nouns(like shade, spirit, vision…) in it, because as a science fiction it were not avoidable. What surprised me was that the author didn’t use a lot of word to explain these nouns. First of all these words are abstract so it make them hard to explain. Some of them are belong to First Nation’s cultures and all kinds of ledgends all over the world these factors make them much harder to explain. The author’s approach can make reader think more about what the words mean. What the author needed to do is to try her best to link the words to the sentences and to make sense. Readers need to understand the sentences and link it to its abstract idea and make their own substantive meaning. It can help readers to understand author’s “spirit world” more deeply. Sharing a example, we know that Cass have got a strong contection to her spirit world so she could feel the diffenece when they passed the boundary. In my opinion, I think that there are fears in her spirit world. She afraided lost Paul and her family everyday in such a dangerous and broken society. That is why she have got strong connection to her spirit world. When they through the boundary, it meant they were safe for a while. Is this can explain why she felt different?
